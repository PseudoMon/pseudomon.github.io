Aliya N. Anindita

Hello! I'm Aliya, a twenty-two-year-old from Jakarta, Indonesia. I'm a fiction writer, a web developer, a translator, and a student of International Relations. This is my homepage, where I store most of my finished work.

On the internet, I'm also known as PseudoMon, PseudoStyian, and Telescopic.

I speak English and Indonesian, and am currently studying Japanese and French.

Other than the above four occupations I read a lot of sciene fiction and fantasy novels, and I also play a ton of video games, with particular interest in indie games, narrative adventures, and RPGs.

You can contact me through my blog, where I discuss the things I like, or my Twitter, where I yell about the things I like. You can also e-mail me with my contact form.

Cheers!

Selected Works

This page contains my favourite works. If you want to get a quick handle of what I can do without having to look through everything in this website, this page is what you’re looking for.

I much prefer writing fiction in English than in Indonesian, but I’m better at translating from English to Indonesian than the other way around.

In terms of programming, my focus right now is on front-end website development. I’m entirely self-taught, but I’m always willing to learn.

Fiction

Skeletons in the Cupboard

A collection of personal short fictions in English, which I've transformed by myself into PDF/MOBI/EPUB and online on the web.

There was a castle at the end of the world where those about to die offer themselves to the voices in the well. They come alone in their rowboats, carrying their most precious possession to be traded with a clean death, free from any form of afterlife.

A short story in English. A slow lamentation with an implicit fantasy lore.
