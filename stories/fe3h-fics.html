FE3H Fanfictions

Aliya N. Anindita
2020/09/14

I do not write fanfic often. Sometimes it makes me feel uncomfortable, as if I’m violating another writer’s story. Of course, fanfics are never anything like that. Whenever we read, play, watch, or experience a story in any way, that story lives in our head, and what lives in our head is ours, in a way. When we let it transforms and grows, branching out of its original author’s intentions, it was us who did the transformation.

The story, stories, of Fire Emblem: Three Houses took root in my head and grow and grow and grow unlike any other. Never before has an existing piece of work pushes me so much to write.

Some of the better fanfics I’ve written I posted on Archive of Our Own. A couple that I’m happy with:

The Wyvern’s Prince is about the relationships between a leader and his pet. Minor spoiler. Readable even for those who have never played the game.

The Masters of the Houses is a Gothic-styled horror AU. While it doesn’t require too much knowledge of the game, it will be better read if you know who Dimitri, Edelgard, and Claude are.

Bows and Arrows is quite possibly the first shippy fic I’ve ever written and also one of those rare works that made me smile as I wrote it. Felix/Bernadetta. Definitely should only be read if you’re familiar with the game and if you’re also into this ship.
