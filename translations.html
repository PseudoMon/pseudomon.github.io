Aliya N. Anindita

Hi! I'm Aliya, a twenty-year-old from Jakarta, Indonesia. I'm currently studying International Relations at school. In my spare time I write stories, code programs and try new things. This is my homepage where I stash them!

I'm also known as PseudoMon, PseudoStygian, and Telescopic.

I speak English and Indonesian, can understand Malay, French, and Sundanese, and am currently learning Japanese

I read a lot, with interest in history both fictional and real, science fiction and fantasy, and weird prose.

I play a lot of games, with particular interest to indie games in general, narrative adventure games, and RPG from either side of the globe.

You can contact me through my blog, my Twitter, or e-mail me with my contact form.

Translations

I'm fluent in both English and Indonesian. I'm a bit more experienced translating nglish to Indonesian than the other way around, but I can do both fairly well.

Other than the list below, I was also one of the contributors for Indonesian translations of Touhou games at Touhou Patch Center. Most of my contributions (translating, proofreading, image-editing) are on Double Dealing Character and Perfect Cherry Blossoms.

Sang Perapian dan Sang Salamander

Indonesian translation of parts of Fahrenheit 415.

Trik Tiga Kartu

Indonesian translation for the first parts of Chandler Groover's Three-Card Trick.

Radio

English translation of a chapter from the novel Ayah by Andrea Hirata.

Kisah November

Indonesian translation of November Tale, from Neil Gaiman's A Calendar of Tales.
