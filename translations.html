Aliya N. Anindita

Hi! I'm Aliya, a twenty-one-year-old from Jakarta, Indonesia. I'm currently studying International Relations at school. In my spare time I write stories, code programs and yell about the things I like. This is my homepage where I stash my work!

I'm also known as PseudoMon, PseudoStygian, and Telescopic.

I speak English and Indonesian, can sorta understand Malay and Sundanese, and currently learning French and Japanese.

The two things I keep throwing my time, money, and thoughts on are books, and video games. I read a lot, with interest in history both fictional and real, science fiction and fantasy, and weird prose. I'll play any video game I can get my hand on, but I have particular interest in indie games, narrative adventure games, and RPG from either side of the globe.

You can contact me through my blog, where I discuss the things i like, my Twitter, where I yell about the things I like, or e-mail me with my contact form.

Translations

I'm fluent in both English and Indonesian. While I'm a bit more experienced translating English to Indonesian than the other way around, I can do both fairly well.

Other than the list below, I was also one of the contributors for Indonesian translations of Touhou games at Touhou Patch Center. Most of my contributions (translating, proofreading, image-editing) are on Double Dealing Character and Perfect Cherry Blossoms.

Dibagi dengan Nol

Indonesian translation of a short story by Ted Chiang.

The Coffee Verse

English translation of a short story by Joko Pinurbo.

A Voice in the Airports

English translation of a short story by Budi Darma.
