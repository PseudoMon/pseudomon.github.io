Aliya N. Anindita

Hello! I'm Aliya, a twenty-one-year-old from Jakarta, Indonesia. I'm a fiction writer, a web developer, a translator, and a student of International Relations. This is my homepage, where I store most of my work.

On the internet, I'm also known as PseudoMon, PseudoStyian, and Telescopic.

I speak English and Indonesian, and am currently studying Japanese and French.

Other than the above four occupations, I'm also an avid reader of novels, mostly science fiction and fantasy, and I also play a ton of video games, with particular interest in indie games, narrative adventures, and RPGs.

You can contact me through my blog, where I discuss the things I like, or my Twitter, where I yell about the things I like. You can also e-mail me with my contact form.

Coding

I'm a self-taught programmer, so my methods are a bit chaotic. I mostly code in Python and Javascript, but I also dabble about in Lua, C++, and PHP. I also know some basic SQL.

Most of my coding work these days is in front-end web development. I prefer to build websites with vanilla HTML/CSS/Javascript, if I can help it, but for more complicated interfaces, I use React or Riot. My preferred back-end, when I need one, is Python with the Flask framework.

I've dabbled about making games with RenPy, Twine, and Love2D.

This site is made by hand with Atom, Babel, IcoMoon, Less CSS, and my own static site generator Plater. It's hosted on Github Pages. Much love to my sister for help designing it.

For my publicly available coding projects, check out my GitHub page.
