Aliya N. Anindita

Hello! I'm Aliya, a twenty-two-year-old from Jakarta, Indonesia. I'm a fiction writer, a web developer, a translator, and a student of International Relations. This is my homepage, where I store most of my finished work.

On the internet, I'm also known as PseudoMon, PseudoStyian, and Telescopic.

I speak English and Indonesian, and am currently studying Japanese and French.

Other than the above four occupations I read a lot of sciene fiction and fantasy novels, and I also play a ton of video games, with particular interest in indie games, narrative adventures, and RPGs.

You can contact me through my blog, where I discuss the things I like, or my Twitter, where I yell about the things I like. You can also e-mail me with my contact form.

Cheers!

Coding

I'm a self-taught programmer, so my methods are a bit chaotic. I mostly code in Python and Javascript, but I've also dabbled about in Lua, C++, and PHP. I also know some basic SQL.

Most of my coding work these days is in front-end web development, but I'm also trying to get into game development.

Web Development

For simple websites, I usually stick with vanilla HTML5/CSS3/ES5 Javacript. My codes target modern browsers, but I try to keep them accessible. I always aim to make my websites mobile-friendly and compatible with accessibility tools.

For more complicated interfaces, I use Riot, React, or Vue. I'm versed enough with modern front-end workflow: NPM, Webpack, Babel, and all.

I've less experience with back-end development. I know how to build a simple one with PHP or Python with the Flask framework. I've previously managed websites built on Wordpress and Simple Machines Forum.

I'm not very good at the visual design part of web development, but it's a field I'm very interested in, so I try my best.

Game Development

I've dabbled about making games with RenPy, Twine, and Love2D. I'm currently making a HTML5 game with PixiJS.
