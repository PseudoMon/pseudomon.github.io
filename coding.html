Aliya N. Anindita

Hi! I'm Aliya, a twenty-year-old from Jakarta, Indonesia. I'm currently studying International Relations at school. In my spare time I write stories, code programs and try new things. This is my homepage where I stash them!

I'm also known as PseudoMon, PseudoStygian, and Telescopic.

I speak English and Indonesian, can understand Malay, French, and Sundanese, and am currently learning Japanese

I read a lot, with interest in history both fictional and real, science fiction and fantasy, and weird prose.

I play a lot of games, with particular interest to indie games in general, narrative adventure games, and RPG from either side of the globe.

You can contact me through my blog, my Twitter, or e-mail me with my contact form.

Coding

I'm a self-taught programmer, so my methods are a bit chaotic. I mostly code in Python and Javascript, but I also have experience with Lua, C++, and PHP. My favourite database system is SQLite.

I prefer to make websites with vanilla Javascript, but for more complicated interfaces, I use React. My preferred back-end is Python with the Flask framework.

I make games with RenPy, Twine, and Love2D.

This site is made by hand with Atom, Babel, IcoMoon, Less CSS, and my own static site generator Plater. It's hosted on Github Pages. Much love to my sister for help designing it.
