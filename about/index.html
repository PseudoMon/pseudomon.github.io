Might you be interested in hearing me talk more about myself?

Aliya is my name, but you can cut it down to Ali or Al or Li if you like. I’m from Jakarta, Indonesia. I’ve spent probably too much of my life online and I’m somewhat active in fandom spaces.

I like languages and cross-cultural communication. I studied International Relations in college, but I’m no longer interested in it professionally. I’m equally fluent in English and Indonesian, absolutely terrible in Sundanese and French, and actively learning Japanese and Chinese.

I play video games of all kind—whether indies or AAA or those in-between—from all corners of the globe. Or I try to, at least. I’m most fond of RPGs, both the JRPG variety and those more directly descended from tabletop; I’m also huge fan of the nascent combatless type.

I like reading novels; mostly fantasy and science fiction with the occasional historical fiction. I’m no good at drawing, but I adore visual art and always do my best to support their artists.

I like bugs despite my arachnophobia (spiders are still cool though). I like birds and funny sea creatures. My favorite extinct animal is the archeopteryx. When making characters based on fauna, my go-to is the lionfish.
