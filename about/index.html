Might you be interested in hearing me talk more about myself?

I use “PseudoMon” as my online alias but at some point I just stick with my first name. It’s Aliya, but you can cut it down to Al or Li if you like. I’m from Jakarta, Indonesia. I’ve spent probably too much of my life online and I’m somewhat active in fandom spaces.

I yap a lot on Twitter, mainly about the games I like. Sometimes I talk about current events too, both local and internationally. I’m very open to meeting random people there! Pop into my mention!

I work as a game programmer in real life, so I care a lot about the state of the industry. I studied International Relations in college; I’m no longer interested in it professionally, but you might catch me musing about it online. I’m equally fluent in English and Indonesian, absolutely terrible in Sundanese and French, and actively learning Japanese and Chinese.

I play video games of all kinds, but nowadays it’s mainly indies, fighting games, and gacha obsessions. I’m very fond of RPGs, both the JRPG variety and those more directly descended from tabletop. I like reading novels too: mostly fantasy and science fiction with the occasional historical fiction.

I like bugs despite my arachnophobia (spiders are still cool though). I like birds and funny sea creatures. My favorite extinct animal is the archeopteryx.

I’m an ace cis woman, but I’m okay with any pronouns.
