writing

I write SF/F short stories and essays about pop culture and the internet.

Most of the stories I’ve written lies somewhere in the science fiction, fantasy, speculative fiction genre. I occassionally write reviews of books and video games and general musings about either, though these days it’s just been limited to unrecorded Twitter threads (whoops).

Works I'm pretty proud of

  • Frida's Songs

    A short story about a soldier and a musician in a nation bent on killing cosmic gods. Interpersonal drama. 9k words. Deicide. In English.

  • The Reclaimed

    A short story with an implicit fantasy lore. 2k words. In English.

  • There’s a Way to make Fleets Work and I Hope Twitter Cares Enough To Make it So

    A blog post about how we use Twitter, how it differs from Instagram, and how its ill-thought-out feature "Fleet" could have worked.

  • Bukan Sekadar Zombi di Kereta

    A review for an indie game, written in Indonesian and featured in Tech in Asia Indonesia, back when they still had a gaming division. Link to external site.

  • Memasuki Dunia Interactive Fiction

    An introduction to Interactive Fiction, a little-known but historic genre of games. Written in Indonesian. Link to external site.
