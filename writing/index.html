Once in a blue moon, I write SF/F stories and blog about pop culture

I haven’t been doing much of that lately, but I sometimes write fiction in the speculative fiction genre. Some are original, some are fan fictions. I also occasionally write reviews of books and video games and general musings about either, although these days it’s just limited to sporadic social media posts.

I write mainly in English, but sometimes also in Indonesian. This site archives most of them.

Works Hightlights

There’s a Way to make Fleets Work and I Hope Twitter Cares Enough To Make it So

A blog post in English about how we use Twitter, how it differs from Instagram, and how its ill-thought-out feature “Fleet” could have worked, at least at the time of writing.

A review for the game The Final Station, written in Indonesian and featured in Tech in Asia Indonesia, back when they still had a gaming division. Link to external site.
