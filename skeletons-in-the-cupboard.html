Skeletons in the Cupboard

Aliya N. Anindita
2019/05/05

Skeletons in the Cupboard is an e-book where I collected the many flash fictions I wrote between 2014 and 2019. Some stories I’ve previously posted on this website, while some are never before seen. In general I’d say this contains my best, but also my most personal super shortform fictions. It’s something I’m proud of but still nervous about sharing.

The e-book is pay-what-you-want (including free) in Itch.io. A web version is also available here.
