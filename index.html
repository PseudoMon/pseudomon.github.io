Aliya N. Anindita

Hello! I'm Aliya, a twenty-one-year-old from Jakarta, Indonesia. I'm a fiction writer, a web developer, a translator, and a student of International Relations. This is my homepage, where I store most of my work.

On the internet, I'm also known as PseudoMon, PseudoStyian, and Telescopic.

I speak English and Indonesian, and am currently studying Japanese and French.

Other than the above four occupations, I'm also an avid reader of novels, mostly science fiction and fantasy, and I also play a ton of video games, with particular interest in indie games, narrative adventures, and RPGs.

You can contact me through my blog, where I discuss the things I like, or my Twitter, where I yell about the things I like. You can also e-mail me with my contact form.
